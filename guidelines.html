Writing Protocol

These standards keep the docs consistent, readable, and highly practical.

01. Direct Tone

Say what to do and explain why. No filler words. Avoid saying "just" or "simply". Define acronyms immediately.

02. Verification

Commands must be copy-pasteable. Every setup guide must end with a Verification section showing expected success states.

03. Tested On

Always log your environment at the top of a tutorial. Example: Ubuntu 22.04, Python 3.10, STS3215 servos.

DANGER: Use this callout style for actions that can cause physical hardware damage or data loss.
